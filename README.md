# Veloura – The Delhi Edit

## AI-Powered Beauty Discovery & Salon Marketplace

Veloura is a modern beauty-tech platform designed to transform how users discover salons, beauty services, and personalized self-care experiences in Delhi.

Instead of browsing endless listings, users receive curated recommendations through an AI-inspired beauty concierge, personalized style profiling, neighborhood-based discovery, and editorial beauty content.

---

## Problem Statement

Finding the right beauty salon or service can be overwhelming.

Users often face:

* Too many choices with little personalization
* Inconsistent reviews and recommendations
* Difficulty finding trusted specialists
* Lack of guidance for beauty and grooming decisions
* No unified platform combining discovery, education, and booking

---

## Solution

Veloura provides a premium beauty marketplace experience that combines:

* Personalized salon discovery
* AI-inspired beauty concierge
* Style preference matching
* Neighborhood-based exploration
* Editorial beauty content
* Seamless appointment booking

The platform helps users discover beauty experiences tailored to their preferences, budget, and location.

---

## Key Features

### AI Beauty Concierge

An intelligent assistant that helps users:

* Discover suitable salons
* Explore treatments
* Get beauty recommendations
* Find services based on occasion and budget

### Style Match Quiz

Interactive beauty profiling system that gathers user preferences and recommends relevant salons and services.

### Neighborhood Discovery

Explore curated beauty destinations across Delhi:

* South Delhi
* Hauz Khas
* Greater Kailash
* West Delhi
* North Delhi

### Curated Salon Marketplace

Users can browse:

* Hair studios
* Makeup artists
* Bridal specialists
* Nail salons
* Grooming centers

### Beauty Journal

Editorial content featuring:

* Beauty trends
* Haircare guides
* Skincare insights
* Bridal beauty inspiration
* Industry recommendations

### User Dashboard

Personalized account experience including:

* Saved salons
* Appointment history
* Upcoming bookings
* Loyalty rewards
* Beauty profile

## User Flow

1. Discover Veloura

4. Explore Salon Profiles
5. Consult AI Concierge
